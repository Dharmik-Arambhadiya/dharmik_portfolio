## ✦ About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer focused on building intuitive digital experiences by understanding how people think, behave, and interact with technology.

At **Redlio Designs**, I've worked on real-world web and product interfaces—designing user flows, creating scalable design systems, and solving complex usability challenges across different domains. My work goes beyond visual design; I focus on aligning user needs with business goals through structured thinking and iterative design.

I'm particularly interested in the intersection of **AI and human-centered design**, exploring how intelligent systems can enhance user experiences while keeping them simple, usable, and meaningful.

With a background in engineering, I bring a balance of design thinking and technical understanding, allowing me to design solutions that are not only visually effective but also practical to implement.
