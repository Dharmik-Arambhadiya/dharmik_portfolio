Case Study
Selected work
Client — Sports Brand Role — Lead Designer Year — 2025

Feel the Game Alive — High-Energy Sports UI

The Problem

Sports brands often struggle to connect with younger audiences through digital interfaces. Traditional sports UI felt dated, lacking the energy and excitement that matches the game-day experience. Users wanted feeling the game on their screens.

The Process

Conducted competitive analysis across major sports platforms. Created moodboards capturing the adrenaline of live matches. Designed with bold typography and dynamic visuals in Figma. Iterated through user feedback to balance visual impact with usability.

The Solution

Delivered a high-energy sports UI featuring bold typography, dynamic hero sections, and an immersive landing page experience. The design captures the raw excitement of football/soccer while maintaining clean, intuitive navigation.

The Outcome

The design gained significant traction on Dribbble's trending page, showcasing the potential for sports brands to elevate their digital presence. The bold visual approach sets a new direction for sports UI design.

Client — HealthTech Startup Role — UI/UX Designer Year — 2025

Human × Machine Sync — Futuristic Web Design

The Problem

Biotech and AI-health companies often present themselves as cold or clinical. This startup wanted to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and human care — showing that AI enhances rather than replaces human touch.

The Process

Explored the intersection of human empathy and machine precision. Used glassmorphism and futuristic aesthetics to convey advanced technology while maintaining warmth. Balanced dashboard functionality with emotional design.

The Solution

Created a futuristic web design featuring glassmorphism effects, gradient-rich interfaces, and a dashboard that feels both advanced and accessible. The design tells the story of human-machine collaboration in healthcare.

The Outcome

The project resonated strongly with the healthtech community, demonstrating that AI-focused products can feel human-centered. The design approach became a reference point for future biotech interfaces.

Client — Smart Home Tech Role — Product Designer Year — 2025

Aiva — AI Smart Home Assistant Landing Page

The Problem

Smart home technology can feel intimidating to average consumers. Aiva needed a landing page that would demystify AI home assistance and make users feel excited about bringing AI into their living spaces.

The Process

Researched consumer pain points around smart home adoption. Focused on creating a warm, welcoming visual language that feels approachable rather than technically overwhelming. Designed clean, minimalist layouts that guide users naturally.

The Solution

Developed a clean, modern landing page design that positions Aiva as a friendly AI companion rather than a technical gadget. The design uses ample whitespace, clear messaging, and intuitive visual hierarchy to convert visitors.

The Outcome

The landing page design helped Aiva connect with broader consumer segments beyond early tech adopters. The clean aesthetic became the foundation for their broader marketing campaign.
